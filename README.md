# Servfix Android Application 📱
ServFix 👷‍♂️🛠️  is your go-to platform for connecting with skilled providers who can handle services like carpentering,  air conditioning repairs, electricity repairs, .. etc. Whether you’re a user seeking top-notch service or a provider looking to showcase your expertise, we’ve got you covered.
# Features

### For Users : 
1. **User Registration and Verification**:
   - New users can sign up by providing essential details, including their mobile number. 📱
   - We ensure security with OTP verification for mobile numbers. 🔒

2. **Choose Your Service Categories**:
   - Explore various service categories—plumbing, electrical, carpentry, and more. 🛠️
   - Select the category that matches your specific needs. 

3. **Post Your Problem**:
   - Describe your issue in detail and attach relevant photos. 📸
   - Choose the appropriate category to ensure the right providers see your post. 📬

4. **Provider Discovery**:
   - Once you've selected a category, explore the profiles of providers in that field. 👀
   - Filter providers by rating, location, and fees to find your ideal match. 🌟
   - Add providers to favourite page 

5. **Chat with Providers**:
   - Click "Send Post" to notify a provider about your problem. 💬
   - Providers receive notifications and can accept or reject the task. 
   - If accepted, chat opens up for detailed discussions. 🗣️

6. **Rate and Review**:
   - After the work is done, rate your provider based on your experience. ⭐
   - Your feedback helps others make informed choices. 🌟

### For Providers:

1. **Expand Your Reach**:
   - Showcase your skills to a wide audience. Gain exposure and attract new clients. 🌐

2. **Manage Conversations**:
   - Respond promptly to user inquiries. Build trust through clear communication. 📲
3. **Accept or Reject Work Requests**:
   
   -When a user sends a specific post or a request to the entire category, providers can accept or reject the task. 🆗❌
3. **Highlight Your Portfolio**:
   - Display your best work. Let your craftsmanship shine! 🎨

4. **Earn Positive Ratings**:
   - Satisfied users mean more business. Deliver exceptional service and watch your reputation grow. 

### Profile Management

- Update your profile picture, address, and other information. 
- Change your password or delete your account if needed. 🔐
- Reset your password easily if you forget it.
  # Technologies and Libraries Used

1. **Kotlin**
   - Primary programming language for Android app development. Offers concise syntax and null safety.

2. **Jetpack Compose**
   - Modern UI toolkit for building native Android user interfaces. Declarative approach simplifies UI development.

3. **MVVM Architecture**
   - Separates UI logic from business logic. Enhances testability and maintainability.

4. **Hilt**
   - Dependency injection library for Android. Provides compile-time safety.

5. **Coil**
   - Image loading library for efficient image loading and caching.

6. **Data Store**
   - Modern data storage solution by Google, replacing SharedPreferences.

7. **Shared Preferences**
   - Stores key-value pairs persistently for small data sets like user preferences.

8. **Lottie Animation**
   - Adds beautiful animations using JSON files exported from Adobe After Effects.

9. **Retrofit**
   - Powerful HTTP client for making network requests. Simplifies API communication and data parsing.

10. **Coroutines**
    - Kotlin's lightweight concurrency framework. Simplifies asynchronous programming.

11. **Firebase**
    - Suite of tools for app development, including authentication, real-time databases, and cloud functions.

12. **Pagination**
    - Essential for efficiently loading large data sets. Ensures smooth scrolling and minimizes memory usage.
